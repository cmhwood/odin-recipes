Sheet Pan Meatloaf

Description

This meatloaf cooks in about 1 hour and has minimal cleanup required. It's perfect for a weeknight meal. Pair with three-ingredient macaroni and cheese, and a vegetable to round out this meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Onion
